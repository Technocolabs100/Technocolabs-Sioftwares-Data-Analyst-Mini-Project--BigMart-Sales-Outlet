Problem Statement :

The aim of the project is to build a predictive model 
that can  predict the sales of each product at a given outlet. 
The model should take into account various factors such as :
item visibility, item prices, location of the outlet, and size of the store. 
The model will help the company to optimize their sales and make informed business decisions.


Hypothesis Generation :

* Item visibility has a significant impact on sales.
* Item prices  play a crucial role in sales.
* location of the outlet have a direct impact on sales.
* Sales are positively correlated with the size of the store.
* Outlets located in urban areas have higher sales compared to those located in rural areas.
